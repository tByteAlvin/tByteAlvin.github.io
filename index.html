Case Study: GreenGift

GreenGift

Eco-friendly drink distribution that turns each box into both a product and a measurable advertising space, with built-in social impact.

Value People get free drinks. Brands get real-world attention.
Revenue Advertising on packaging + street-level brand interaction.
Impact Tree planting + help for leukemia patients + homeless support.

Why it works

  • Physical distribution creates unavoidable visibility.
  • Distributors introduce the brand and run quick surveys.
  • Eco packaging reduces plastic use and signals sustainability.

What I’m building

  • A repeatable distribution system.
  • A pricing model based on box sides + volume.
  • A reporting layer: QR + survey + results dashboard.
